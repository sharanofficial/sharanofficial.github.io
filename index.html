Quality technology for better healthcare

We are a team of young developers developing digital products and services that have a positive impact on people. We work exclusively with Mindwave Ventures based in London, UK.

services

We are a passionate and energetic team of designers and developers committed to creating impactful digital products and services. We work exclusively with Mindwave Ventures to develop for their clients.

design

We turn the designs of the Mindwave team into front-end code for real-life applications. Mindwave focuses on user-centered designs, ensuring that the platforms we build have a positive impact. We work with CMS such as Wagtail and Apostrophe.

development

A team of talented full-stack developers working on both the front-end and back-end programming of a range of projects to connect clinicians, patients, carers and researchers, while aiming to bring technology and healthcare together todo things better.

virtual reality

We develop prototype VR experiences for health research projects. These include an experience for the treatment of OCD in young people and an intervention for people within the addiction and forensic services.

featured works

The team has worked on various projects with Mindwave Ventures whose clients include the National Health Service in the UK, charities and private sector organisations.

personal health record projects

Mediwave has been developing over 10 Personal Health Record projects (PHR) in for NHS Trusts across Worcester, Cheshire & Merseyside and a couple of others in UK. PHRs allow users to access their physical and mental health data and keep track of their daily activities like sleep, mood etc. Users can also manage their appointments and check prescriptions

research projects

We have been designing and developing various applications to support research conducted by clients like King's college london, Thalamos, London CCG's, South london and maudsley NHS foundation trust and the like.

health vr projects

We have been creating VR experiences using physiological tracking tests to give evidence-based therapy to those suffering from pyromania, drug addiction, phobia of germs and other similar phobias to help them feel a bit more prepared to face real life.

about our company

Mediwave Digital is the dedicated development arm of Mindwave Ventures which works on bringing together quality development and good design to deliver digital products and services.

Mindwave Ventures was founded by Kumar Jacob who has worked in technology and healthcare for around 20 years. He is also a Founder director of Dream Reality Interactive - a VR and AR game developer. Kumar has a strong background in healthcare, having served on the board of the NHS Trust and charities, He helped set up SH:24, an online sexual health service where he is a non-executive director.

Kumar set up Mediwave to be the dedicated development arm of Mindwave. Found a team of young passionate developers in Pondicherry, India. The team continues to grow.

Mediwave Team has so far worked on websites, apps, VR projects like Healthlocker, GoodThinking, Medichec, Health VR for the NHS and leading academic research centres in London and across the UK. The company provides great working atmosphere. Learning and growth is a continuous process at Mediwave. All employees benefit from excellent Medical Insurance, premiums for which fully paid by the company.
